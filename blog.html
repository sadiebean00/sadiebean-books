Writing Tips

How I create suspense and cozy vibes in my mysteries.

One of my favourite techniques is layering small clues throughout a story while keeping the focus on the characters. Building atmosphere can be just as important as plotting the mystery itself. I have my own board that you would typically see in your favourite murder mystery shows!

Behind the Scenes

A peek into my writing process and inspiration.

From handwritten notes to digital outlines, every book starts with a simple idea. Inspiration often comes from places I've visited, conversations I've overheard, or mysteries I've always wanted to solve. When writing, I often listen to music from my favourite movies or shows to get me in the mood.
